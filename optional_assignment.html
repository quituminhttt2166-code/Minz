According to the assignment instructions, students may upload a short presentation video with a duration of less than three minutes. The video should briefly explain the topic or summarize the main ideas.

In addition, students are required to write a short note in the comments section describing the content of their presentation.

3. Why No Video Was Submitted

For this submission, no video presentation was uploaded. However, the assignment instructions were carefully reviewed and fully understood.

4. Key Points Learned
